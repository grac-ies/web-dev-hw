first make the dough!
you will need:

  • 1/4 cup of yeast
  • one cup of oil
  • one bag of crust mix
  • one huge bag of flour
  • and two buckets of water

pizza dough directions

  • combine all ingrediants except flour and violently whisk
  • wait five min
  • add flour and mix in your huge resturant grade mixer with dough hook attatchment
  • let rest for another 10 min
  • for a larger measure out 19.2oz
  • let rest, preferably up to three hours

making the pizza

  • cover dough in a flour and corn meal mixture
  • roll out with rolling pin from center out
  • if bold place two hands undert the dough an inch and a half away from the edge and throw using more of one hand than the other
  • flour and place on screen
  • add sauce using ladle
  • pour ladle of sauce in the center of the dough
  • in circular motions staring from the center spread the sauce starting with more preasure in the center and less as you move outwards
  • add small layer of cheese and any toppings you may want
  • place the pizza in a pizza oven being sure to spin half way through
  • no set time on when your pizza will be done depending on when the oven was turneed on
  • take pizza out and place in box
  • cut the pizza in half then in half again then twice through each quarter
