ingredients

  • 3 roma tomatoes diced
  • 2 avocados diced
  • 1/3 cup red onion diced
  • 15oz black beans drained
  • 15oz black eyed peas drained
  • 1 1/2 cup sweet corn
  • 1 bell pepper diced
  • 1/3 cup cilantro
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon peppwe
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • tortilla chips for serving

directions

  1. combine tomatos, avocado, onion, black beans, black eyed peas, orn, pepper ,and cilantro in a large bowl
  2. toss well
  3. in a seperate bowl whisk together olive oil, lime juice, red wine vinegar, sugar, salt, pepper, and garlic powder
  4. pour dressing over veggies and toss
  5. keep refrigerated until serving
